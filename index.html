News

As F. Scott Fitzgerald would say, "life starts over when it gets crisp in the fall." That is exactly what is happening now as the Tartan Wind Ensemble is starting their 2nd full year with a great rehearsal. Things to look forward to in the new year include a great new set of music, plenty of new members, and wonderful new things such as breathing exercies, set playlists, and of course this cool website. Rehearsals this semester will be Wednesdays from 6pm-8pm and Sundays from 10:30am to 1pm. Here's to an amazing new year!
Director of Performance Karina Chiu has been hard at work on crafting a new fall set this summer. Look forward to a challenging, and exciting fall that includes works by Holst, Holsinger and others. Look for the full set list to be put out later in the year as its finalized.
All of our officers have been hard at work getting ready for an amazing fall semester. We are excited to have this year be our first with actual funding, and our own closet. We also are so excited to see all of our members here again and create some great music.
