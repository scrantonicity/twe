About Us

The Tartan Wind Ensemble is a musical ensemble at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. It provides an opportunity for any person affiliated with the University, including but not limited to students, staff, faculty, or alumni, to rehearse and perform in a wind ensemble in a recreational manner with no auditions.

How to Join


1. You are sad. The lack of music in your life is eating away at you. All you want is a stress free group where you can express your inner musician.


2. You decide to come to a TWE rehearsal which are either on Saturday mornings or Tuesday nights. Once you show up you just start playing with either your own instrument or a rented one since there are no auditions.


3. You are happy now with TWE in your life. You know you'll never have the stress of auditioning, and you can miss rehearsals now and then without any reprecussions. Plus you get the thrill of performing in a concert once a semester.

FAQ

Watching TWE

  • When can I hear TWE perform?

    There is one concert per semester performed at the end of the semester. Check our calendar or facebook page for event information on details.

  • What will TWE be performing at their next concert?

    Our concert pieces can change throughout the semester, but the current list of pieces will be on the front page of the website once decided upon each semsester.

  • Does it cost anything to attend the concert?

    The concerts are completely free and have no tickets. Just show up to our concert venue and enjoy the show.
