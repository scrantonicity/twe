About Us

The Tartan Wind Ensemble is a musical ensemble at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. It provides an opportunity for any person affiliated with the University, including but not limited to students, staff, faculty, or alumni, to rehearse and perform in a wind ensemble in a recreational manner with no auditions.

FAQ

Watching TWE

TWE has two concerts a year, at the end of both the Fall and Spring semesters. For current dates and times check the homepage of this website, or our facebook page .
Yes you can! Each concert is livestreamed on our facebook page . Just go to our page at the time of the concert and you should be able to view the whole thing. You can also view past concerts from our facebook page as well.
The concert is always and forever will be absolutely free of charge!

Joining TWE

Wow first off we are so excited you are considering joining us! To join simply show up to one of our rehearsals which can be found on our calendar. We will have music for you to play, and accept all skill levels.
Nope! Any musician is welcome to join, and there are never any auditions.
We do have instruments that are available to rent out for the semester. These are available in limited supply, but it is very likely we can get you an instrument to play.

The Tartan Wind Ensemble was founded in the Spring of 2017, with our first concert coming in Fall 2017. Founding officers included E.J. Dorsey, Dylan Hyun, Becky Jesurum, and Michael Spoerl.
At the end of the Fall Semester elections are held to determine officers for the next two semesters. Any member is welcome to run. Scroll down to see a list of current officers and positions.
