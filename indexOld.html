News

We are excited to announce that the annual Fall Concert will be on Saturday November 17th at 7 pm in the CUC Studio Theatre. We are excited to see everyone there and as always admission is free. Find the livestream on our Facebook page.
As F. Scott Fitzgerald would say, "life starts over when it gets crisp in the fall." That is exactly what is happening now as the Tartan Wind Ensemble is starting their 2nd full year with a great rehearsal. Things to look forward to in the new year include a great new set of music, plenty of new members, and wonderful new things such as breathing exercies, set playlists, and of course this cool website. Rehearsals this semester will be Wednesdays from 6pm-8pm and Sundays from 10:30am to 1pm. Here's to an amazing new year!Ï
Performance Director Karina Chiu has been hard at work on crafting a new fall set this summer. Look forward to a challenging, and exciting fall that includes works by Holst, Holsinger and others. Look for the full set list to be put out later in the year as its finalized.
